Plant Overview

Understanding different plant categories

Indoor Plants

Perfect for brightening your indoor spaces. These plants are adapted to thrive in controlled environments and help improve air quality.

  • Low to medium light requirements
  • Easy to maintain
  • Air-purifying benefits
  • Variety of sizes and styles

Outdoor Plants

Robust plants designed for gardens, balconies, and outdoor spaces. They bring nature closer and create beautiful landscapes.

  • Full sun to partial shade
  • Weather-resistant varieties
  • Seasonal blooming options
  • Attracts beneficial wildlife

Succulents

Stylish, low-maintenance plants with thick, fleshy leaves. Perfect for modern decor and busy lifestyles.

  • Minimal watering needed
  • Unique shapes and colors
  • Drought-tolerant
  • Great for small spaces

Flowering Plants

Beautiful plants that add color and fragrance to any space. They bring joy with their vibrant blooms and sweet scents.

  • Vibrant colors and fragrances
  • Seasonal or year-round blooming
  • Attracts pollinators
  • Creates cheerful atmosphere

Care Instructions

Essential tips for healthy plants

🌞

Light & Location

Place plants in appropriate light conditions. Most indoor plants prefer bright, indirect light while outdoor plants need direct sunlight.

💧

Watering

Water plants when the top inch of soil feels dry. Overwatering is the most common cause of plant death.

🌿

Maintenance

Regularly clean leaves, fertilize during growing seasons, and repot when roots become crowded.
